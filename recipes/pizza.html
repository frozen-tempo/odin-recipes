Pizza

A classic pepperoni pizza with a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and plenty of pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 jar pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil
  • Dried oregano
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the pizza dough package.
  2. Prepare the dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Add toppings: Place the dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough. Top with mozzarella cheese and pepperoni.
  4. Bake: Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with oregano and garlic powder. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
