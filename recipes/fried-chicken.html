Fried Chicken

Crispy, golden-brown fried chicken with a flavorful coating.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Brine the chicken: Submerge the chicken pieces in buttermilk and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight.
  2. Prepare the coating: In a shallow dish, combine the flour, salt, pepper, and paprika.
  3. Coat the chicken: Remove the chicken pieces from the buttermilk and coat them in the flour mixture.
  4. Fry the chicken: Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C). Fry the chicken pieces for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Drain and serve: Remove the fried chicken from the oil and drain on paper towels. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
