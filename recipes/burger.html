Burger

A classic American burger, featuring a juicy beef patty, crispy bacon, melted cheese, and fresh lettuce, tomato, and onion, all served on a toasted bun.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 slice bacon
  • 1 slice cheese (your choice)
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 1 tomato slice
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bun, toasted

Steps

  1. Prepare the patty: In a bowl, combine the ground beef, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Shape into a patty and set aside.
  2. Cook the bacon: Cook the bacon in a skillet until crispy. Drain on paper towels and set aside.
  3. Cook the patty: Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the patty for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  4. Assemble the burger: Place the cooked patty on the bottom bun. Top with cheese, bacon, lettuce, tomato, and onion. Place the top bun on top and serve.
